Our Services

A complete suite of services to make your dream wedding a reality.

Destination Wedding

Picture yourselves in a warm, luxurious atmosphere with happy friends and family toasting the two of you after recuperating from travel time. Having a wedding away from home is attractive for different reasons, and the phrase 'Destination Weddings' itself creates excitement. The best part is that families get more time together, and only your near and dear ones gather to celebrate. To manage it beautifully, our experienced team handles everything from venue selection to curating all essentials for the wedding.

Wedding Planning

A wedding is the most important time of your life, but it also brings a lot of responsibilities and stress. There are tons of things that you have to manage, from small details to major arrangements. We take that stress away, providing full-service planning that covers every aspect of your event. Our meticulous planning ensures that you can relax and fully immerse yourself in the joy of the occasion, confident that every detail is being handled by professionals.

Venue Selection

As planners, we are much more familiar with the capabilities of a space, the layout, and the time and items you'll need to really transform it. We see the potential and can match a venue to your vision and budget. We help you select the perfect backdrop for your story, considering factors like capacity, ambiance, and logistics to ensure the setting is as magical as the moment itself.

Pre-Wedding Rituals

An Indian wedding is a sweet concoction of traditions, values, and rituals that are not just beautiful to look at but have a deeper meaning. These ceremonies are a vital part of the celebration, bringing families together and marking the beginning of a new chapter. We help organize and coordinate these events, from the Roka to the Mehendi and Sangeet, ensuring each ritual is performed beautifully and respectfully.

Wedding Entertainment

A crucial mistake some couples make is underestimating the importance of the entertainment on their special day. Whether it's a DJ, a live band, or traditional performers, the right entertainment sets the mood for the entire event. We help you hire the perfect artists who align with your taste and can keep your guests engaged and celebrating all night long.

Wedding Decor

No Indian wedding is ever complete without elaborate wedding decorations. Colorful Indian weddings require proper preparation, planning, and coordination. From breathtaking floral arrangements and mandaps to elegant lighting and tablescapes, our design team works with you to create a cohesive and stunning visual experience that will leave a lasting impression on you and your guests.
