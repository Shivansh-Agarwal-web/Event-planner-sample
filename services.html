Our Wedding Services

A complete suite of services to make your dream Indian wedding a reality.

Destination Weddings

Imagine a royal wedding in a Jaipur palace or a serene ceremony on the beaches of Goa. The concept of a 'Destination Wedding' creates excitement and allows families to bond. The best part is the intimate gathering of your closest friends and family. To manage this beautifully, our experienced team handles everything from venue selection to curating all essentials for your special days.

Full Wedding Planning

An Indian wedding is a grand affair with numerous responsibilities. From vendor negotiations and budget management to guest hospitality and logistics, the tasks can be overwhelming. We take that stress away, providing full-service planning that covers every aspect of your event. Our meticulous planning ensures you can relax and fully immerse yourself in the joy of your wedding celebrations.

Venue Selection

As planners, we are familiar with the capabilities of different spaces, from grand banquet halls to lush farmhouses. We understand the layout, logistics, and time needed to transform a venue. We help you select the perfect backdrop for your story, considering factors like capacity for all your functions, ambiance, and budget to ensure the setting is as magical as the moment.

Pre-Wedding Rituals

An Indian wedding is a beautiful concoction of traditions, values, and rituals that have deep meanings. These ceremonies are a vital part of the celebration. We help organize and coordinate these events, from the Roka and Sagan to the Mehendi and Sangeet night, ensuring each ritual is performed beautifully and respectfully, creating joyful memories for everyone involved.

Wedding Entertainment

A crucial mistake is underestimating the importance of entertainment. Whether it's a celebrity DJ for the cocktail party, a live band for the reception, or traditional folk dancers for the Mehendi, the right entertainment sets the mood. We help you hire the perfect artists who align with your taste and can keep your guests engaged and celebrating all night long.

Wedding Decor & Design

No Indian wedding is complete without elaborate decorations. Colorful celebrations require proper preparation, planning, and coordination. From breathtaking floral mandaps and royal entryways to elegant lighting and themed tablescapes, our design team works with you to create a cohesive and stunning visual experience that will leave a lasting impression.
